Meet Josh Stuart aka RedBeard! His Bio, Age, Net Worth, Wife

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard was one of the co-founders of Diesel Sellerz. He appeared in the early seasons of Diesel Brothers but disappeared from the reality show once season 5 debuted in 2019.

So, what is he doing now? What’s his age, and net worth, and who’s his wife? Find answers as this Josh Stuart aka RedBeard Bio proceeds.

Diesel Brothers: What Happened To Josh Stuart aka RedBeard?

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard is alive and doing well. Though we may not see him on the Discovery show, he’s definitely still a part of the Diesel Brothers family.

In case you haven’t been following him on IG, he’s done several giveaways with products from Diesel Power Gear, and he’s even participated in a few Instagram unboxing posts, after disappearing from the show. So, ease up, he might have just limited how much filming he is taking part in.

Infact, at the time of writing this article, Josh still had the show’s name listed in his Instagram bio. Also, he didn’t seem to have bad blood with the remaining cast members as he still followed both Diesel Dave and Heavy D on Instagram.

RedBeard’s last appearance on the show was in 2019. So far, he’s graced over 4 eps of Diesel Brothers.

Now, the show’s on its season 8.

“Guess who’s back!? Tonight is the night my friends!! @diesel.brothers starts in about 40 minutes!! Better get some snacks, tune in to @discovery and get comfy,” Diesel Dave posted on IG.

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard Age

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard was 40 years of age in 2022.

He is younger than both Diesel Brothers.

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard Family

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard comes from a family of at least five.

His mother, Pamela Stuart @pamela.stuart.73307 lived in Billings, MT.

As for his brother, Ryan Jerald Stuart (born: Aug 1987), he is an Orem, Utah resident who worked as a Graphic designer at “GroupRateIt.com.” Previously, he used to work at “Dieselsellerz.com.”

Likewise, his sister, Mandi Stuart (born: Jul 1981) lived in Salt Lake City, Utah. She is a Salt Lake Community College, University of Phoenix, and Union High graduate who went on to join Marine Corps Base Quantico. Thereafter, she worked as an American Guard at Coastal International Security, a Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) at Cornerstone Aviation, and  Airline Pilot/First Officer at Envoy Air – American Airlines.

“The quickest way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the exploration of the world,” Mandi said.

Sadly, Josh’s maternal grandfather, Jerald Petersen passed away on November 14, 2018, at the age of 82. He was a devout member and priesthood holder in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints and a father of eight.

Does Josh Stuart aka RedBeard Work For The Diesel Brothers?

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard doesn’t work for The Diesel Brothers, he works with The Diesel Brothers!

He is the co-founder/CFO/COO of Diesel Power Gear, Diesel Trucks For Sale, and Diesel Sellerz. No wonder, he gained a reputation as a skilled negotiator and a money-hungry businessman.

Also, the guy is the owner of Legacy Investing and the founder of Hybrid Pest Control.

Prior to starting his own company, Josh worked as a “Sales Manager / Trainer” at Protection 1 Security Solutions, “Sales Rep / Trainer” at Elite Security Services, “Sales Rep” at APX Alarm Security Solutions Inc, and “Sales Manager” at Firstline Security, combined for over 7 years.

For those who don’t know, Diesel Sellerz is the world’s largest Diesel marketplace where you can buy and sell diesel-powered gear. “Whether you’re a private seller, a dealer, or in the market for your own diesel beast, you’ve come to the right place,” their official site wrote.

As for his exclusive work with the Diesel Brothers, Josh has helped the two stars in restoring and re-opening Maple River Wellness Springs by the Oneida Reservoir. 

How Much Is Josh Stuart aka RedBeard Net Worth?

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard flaunted a net worth of $900 thousand in 2022.

On the other hand, Heavy D and Diesel Dave are worth over twice as much at $2 million each.

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard Wife

Josh Stuart aka RedBeard was married to his wife April Stuart on May 2, 2008, and it was his greatest accomplishment. “Talking a super hot, amazing woman into marrying me. I also got four crazy cool kids out of the deal,” he once said.

Also, on their 12 anniversary, Josh took it to his IG to share, “Props to @casa.de.stuart for making it 12 yrs with me. Lord knows I haven’t made it easy…and not only has she hung in there but she’s done it with style and grace.”

Praising her mother, the star added, “She is by far my best friend in the world….there’s nobody I’d rather spend my time with. She’s a fantastic mother, daughter, friend, partner, and overall stellar human being.”

As for their kids, Josh and April share four — Rad, Wolfgang, Luna, and Soleil. They were in 7th, 6th, 4th, and 2nd grade respectively in 2022.

Now, talking about his wife, April (born: Mar 29, 1981) is a florist. She makes intuitive floral designs for Ceremonial and Scared spaces. Check her designs on IG @casa.de.stuart.

On April’s 39th birthday, Josh posted an IG pic of her, captioned, “She is by far my favorite person in the world. She’s the most fun, thoughtful, caring, hardworking, badasses around. She’s one of those rare souls who many claims as their “best friend”. I love you, happy birthday.”
