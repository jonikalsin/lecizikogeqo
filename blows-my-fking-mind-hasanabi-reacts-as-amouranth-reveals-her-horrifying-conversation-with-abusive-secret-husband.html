Blows my f**king mind: HasanAbi reacts as Amouranth reveals her horrifying conversation with a

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 19, 2024

Amouranth, a well-known Twitch streamer, recently opened up about her ‘secret husband’. On October 16, the popular streamer had an emotional outburst after speaking on the phone with her spouse live on the stream. The streamer and her husband could be seen sharing a disturbing conversation, during which he threatened to murder their dogs and take all the money the popular Twitch star had earned.

The video of the disputed stream cannot be accessed on Amouranth’s Twitch account, however, the footage of the streamer visibly discussing the harassment she has had and is still experiencing has been circulating on social media.

In one footage, the streamer confronts her husband about why he claimed he was going to murder their dogs. To this he responds, “Leave the home,” before getting angry, aggressive, and accusing the streamer of lying.

Hasanabi shares his utter shock at Amouranth’s abusive husband

Like many other streamers in the industry, Hasanabi also took to his social media to share his thoughts about Amouranth’s unfortunate situation. On his YouTube channel, Hasanabi revealed that he had been awoken by fellow streamers to check out Amouranth’s stream when the incident took place. While the VOD is now deleted, there are several excerpts of the disturbing conversation now doing the rounds on the internet.

In one of the most disturbing clips, when Amouranthconfronted him, she admitted that he had coerced her into running a constant livestream. After hearing the remark, the husband exploded, saying:

“No! I did not say that! Now you’re just f***ing being a liar! I said, ‘I’m going to tell you what I said, in terms of the earlier!’ You’re saying you did not say that! And then you tell me, ‘What did you say?'”           

To this HasanAbi reacted saying that he has never seen anything to this degree of domestic violence. He said, “This person is like so insane. The text messages, the things that he is like so comfortable stating, like it blows my f**king mind that he is so comfortable in abusing and exploiting Amouranth.”

A few while after the phone call, Amouranth revealed that her husband had sent her threatening texts. She disclosed communications in which he threatened to discard her belongings and goods if she did not reply to his messages.

When asked about her financial condition, Amouranth revealed that her husband was in charge of all of their accounts. She stated that he had threatened to steal the remainder and left her with just $1 million.

She also stated that it was her husband’s orders that prevented her from previously disclosing her marriage. It was supposedly kept secret because he told her it would destroy their “business model.”

Many Twitch streamers and other internet personalities have voiced their concerns for Amouranth with respect to her abusive husband.

Daph, in a tweet, expressed  that she is genuinely terrified for Amouranth and hopes she is okay.

Many other streamers like Valkyrae, Pokimane, and others have reached out to Amouranth about her well being, even going to the extent of sending the police to her house to ensure her safety and security. Fans can only hope that with the situation being out in the open now, the popular OnlyFans star will gain the courage to leave the abusive environment she has been subjected to for the past seven years.
