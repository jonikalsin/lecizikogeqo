Who is Singer and Artist Amanda Hawkins? Explore Stanley Cup National Anthem To Be Sung By Her

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Who is Singer and Artist Amanda Hawkins? Amanda Hawkins, an American singer and performer, performed the national anthem at the 2022 Stanley Cup Finals.

Amanda grew up in the city of Denver, Colorado. At the age of 11, she began playing the guitar and singing. At 2007, she enrolled in a music school. Since she was a teenager, she has been singing on stages and writing and publishing music for her audience.

Viewers were in awe of the lovely singer’s voice after she approached the stage to perform the national anthem before Game 1 of the Stanley Cup Finals. The national anthem singer’s performance on stage has gotten a lot of attention on Twitter.

Let’s delve deeper into the life of Amanda Hawkins, a remarkable singer.

Who is Singer and Artist Amanda Hawkins?

Hawkins is a Denver-native singer, songwriter, and artist. In her professional career, she has sung songs like ‘Heaven or Hell’, ‘Call You’, ‘Save My Soul’, ‘I Love You Already’, ‘The Way You Feel’, ‘Get back In Line’, and so on.

She reported to the Westworld, “Music has been my life since I was eleven. It’s in my blood. It keeps me sane. It’s how I express myself.”

Amanda has grown up idolizing singers from across the globe and it is reflected in her singing style. She took inspiration from artists like Lauryn Hill, Janis Joplin, Mariah Carey, Bruce Springsteen, and Crosby. She has sang R&B-style songs when she was 15 years old. She slowly drifted towards hip-hop singing.

In December 2015, she decided to take a short break from music as she was pregnant. In 2017, she became the talk of the town when she sang Christmas songs on loudspeakers at Denver International Airport.

In 2019 she was blessed with her second daughter and she was not seen on the stage. After taking a break of nearly eight years to raise her two daughters, she returned back to the music industry with her song ‘Trust You’re There’.

Stanley Cup National Anthem

The talented singer astonished the audience by singing the national anthem for Colorado Avalanche, Denver Nuggets, Colorado Rapids, Colorado Crush, Colorado Mammoth, and Susan G. Foundation.

For the Stanley Cup Finals 2022 where Avalanche will be seen against Tampa Bay Lightning, Hawkins inaugurated the game by singing this year’s national anthem in support of the Avalanche.

Was Amanda Hawkins in American Idol?

In order to pursue her musical career, Amanda went to Los Angeles in 2006. While attending a music institute there in Los Angeles, she went to audition for the American Idol.

The singer participated as a contestant in season 7 of the American Idol. She made it to the top fifties before getting eliminated. Although her audition was not broadcasted on television, she gave her audition in San Diego and was cut off before making it to the top 24.

The Colorado singer always knew that it was her dream to be a singer and songwriter and she was not ready to give up easily. She moved back to Denver and started to perform on stage and released her solo songs.
