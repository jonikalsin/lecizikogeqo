Bio of Lala Kent 

The popular model, Lala Kent was born on 2 September 1990 in Salt Lake City, Utah, The USA, under the birth name, Lauryn Kent. She was born to father Kent Burningham and mother Lisa Burningham and grew up along with her brother, Easton Burningham. In addition, she belongs to white ethnicity and has an American nationality. Moreover, her sign of birth is Virgo.

Furthermore, she has not revealed anything about her educational qualifications to the public. Lala initially started her career as a hostess at a restaurant, SUR in California. Likewise, she professionally started her career as a model by appearing in the music videos. Similarly, she has appeared in several low budget movies.

Moreover, she started appearing in some major and minor roles in movies which include Dudes & Dragons, Pitching Love, and Catching Faith. To date, she has appeared over 15+ movies such as The Row (2018), One Shot, The MentorVault, Axis Sally.

However, she rose to fame when she appeared in the Bravo television series, Vanderpump Rules. As a model, she was featured on the cover of Women's Fitness magazine for their March/April issue in 2019.

Personal Life: Partner

The 29 years old model, Lala Kent is engaged to film producer, Randall Emmett as of 2019. The two of them engaged on 1 September 2018 after Randall proposed her on a trip to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. As per some sources, the two of them started dating in 2016.

As per some sources, she was previously in a relationship with James Kennedy for a year. On the other hand, Randall was already married to an actress, Ambyr Childers and shares two daughters, Rylee and London with each other.
