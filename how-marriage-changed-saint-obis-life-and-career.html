How Marriage Changed Saint Obis Life and Career

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 24, 2024

After the death of actor Saint Obi, movie producer Zik Zulu Okafor shared how Obi’s marriage impacted his life and career.

In Okafor’s article, he disclosed that the actor’s wife, who was a top executive in a telecommunications company, caused strain in his relationships with friends and his acting career.

Okafor revealed that the actor’s health declined roughly a year before his passing.

Below is an excerpt from the article:

“Saint Obi, real name Obinna Nwafor, was known for his shy and reserved nature. However, his marriage drastically altered his lifestyle and separated him from the creative community that propelled his career in Nollywood. His marriage not only distanced him from his friends but also led him to withdraw from acting, abandoning his previous aspirations.”

Until his passing, Saint struggled with the consequences of his marriage, his declining health, and the challenges of solitary living away from his family and former life in the limelight.

His story serves as a reminder of the price of fame and the personal sacrifices that actors often endure.
