Kelly Roseberry Obituary, A Precious Soul Gone Too Soon

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Kelly Roseberry Obituary, Death –The Travis Mills Foundation, a renowned non-profit organization dedicated to supporting post-9/11 veterans and their families, is grappling with a profound loss. The passing of Kelly Roseberry, the foundation’s Vice President of Programming, has left an indelible void in the organization. Kelly’s unwavering dedication, boundless passion, and genuine warmth touched the lives of everyone who had the privilege of knowing her. This tribute is a testament to the extraordinary legacy she leaves behind.

Kelly Roseberry was a steadfast and passionate leader who served the Travis Mills Foundation from its inception in 2013. As the Vice President of Programming, her role was instrumental in developing and overseeing the programs and services offered to veterans and their families at the foundation’s retreat in the serene Belgrade Lakes Region of Maine.

Kelly’s contributions to the Travis Mills Foundation were immeasurable. She was a driving force behind the foundation’s mission to provide a healing environment for wounded veterans and their families. Her dedication was evident in the way she tirelessly worked to create programs and services that would empower veterans to overcome the physical and emotional challenges they faced after returning from combat.

Under Kelly’s leadership, the foundation’s retreat offered a wide range of activities, including adaptive sports, family gatherings, and support groups. These programs were designed not only to aid in physical recovery but also to foster camaraderie and emotional healing among the veterans and their loved ones. Kelly’s innovative thinking and tireless efforts expanded the foundation’s reach, ensuring that it continued to be a beacon of hope for those who had sacrificed so much for their country.

Kelly Roseberry’s connection to the military community ran deep. She came from a military family, with several relatives who had served in the armed forces. This familial link gave her a unique perspective on the sacrifices made by those in uniform. It fueled her determination to make a difference in the lives of post-9/11 veterans and their families.

Kelly’s genuine empathy and understanding of the challenges faced by veterans made her approachable and relatable. She had an innate ability to connect with the people she served, making them feel understood and supported. This personal connection allowed her to develop programs that catered to the specific needs of the veteran community, addressing the often-overlooked emotional and psychological aspects of their journey.

In addition to her profound commitment to veterans and their families, Kelly Roseberry was no stranger to adversity. In 2019, she was diagnosed with breast cancer, a battle that she fought and won. Her victory over this challenging period in her life served as an inspiration to those around her. It showcased her resilience, determination, and unwavering spirit in the face of adversity.

Kelly’s experience with cancer provided her with a deeper understanding of the power of hope and the importance of a support network during tough times. She channeled this knowledge into her work at the Travis Mills Foundation, ensuring that the programs she developed were not only about physical healing but also about fostering a sense of hope and resilience in the face of life’s difficulties.

Kelly Roseberry’s legacy at the Travis Mills Foundation is profound and enduring. The programs and services she developed continue to make a tangible difference in the lives of countless veterans and their families. Her work has helped veterans regain their independence, restore their self-esteem, and rebuild their lives after enduring the physical and emotional scars of war. The foundation’s retreat in the Belgrade Lakes Region stands as a testament to Kelly’s unwavering commitment to creating a haven of healing and support. It remains a place where veterans can find solace and build lifelong connections with others who share their experiences.

Kelly’s impact also extends to her colleagues and the broader community. Her warmth, kindness, and dedication have left an indelible mark on those who had the privilege of working alongside her.
