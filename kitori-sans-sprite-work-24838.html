Kitori-San's Sprite Work | Bulbagarden

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 24, 2024
Well, seeing everyone making sprites and all, it inspired me to do a few of my transpecies characters and some human sprites.

Q. Transpec-WHUT?
A. Transpecies. I dunno if my friend made that word up when she was suggesting names for my race of human Pokemon or what. Anyways, Transpecies are (mostly) escaped projects from Cipher. They humans who posses traits of a specific pokemon and can transform into that Pokemon. The deal with the transformation is that the...errrr... here, let's put it this way.

Charizard Transpecies (Human Form)+ Contact with large douse of fire= Charizard
Charizard Transpecies (Charizard Form)+ Contact with large douse of fire= Human

You has moar question? You PM Kitori-San. Stick Finger in Light Socket. Be enlightened.

ANYHOO... THIS.IS.SPRITE WORK! *BOOT TO THE GUT*

Akane the Flareon


This is the Middle child of my Eevee Sisters. She is rather short tempered and usually picks on her little sister, Kagami (Jolteon.) She is around 26 years old and serves as a waitress.

Kagami the Jolteon


This is the youngest of my Eevee Sisters. She is a spunky young girl who lives in the outskirts of Vermillion City. She is about 23 years old. Character created by a friend of mine, however she had me design her.

Atari the Vaporeon (No, she's not a gaming system, nor is she related to one.)


Atari is an elegant young woman and is the eldest of my Eevee Sisters. She speaks so elegantly that it annoys the crap out of her little sisters, especially Kagami, when she seeks her help. She is 28 years old.

Taya Moonshin the Salamance


Taya recieved her first pokemon, a Chickorita, and began her pokemon journey at nineteen years old. However, she lost her pokemon when a young man named Taniko crashed into her when chasing a Cipher member named Dakim/Dakimu. She was kidnapped on the same day to become a Salamance transpecies. She loves to wear bright and annoying colors most times and refuses to leave Taniko's side until he finds her Chickorita. A character made by another friend and was designed by me (At least the transpecies edition was.) She is 19 years old.

Ayame Takashi, the Latios


Ayame is the guardian of the red orb of Hoenn and partner of Kirika Hushima (Latias transpecies.) Ayame is arrogant and short fused, but when the going gets tough, he will do anything to complete his job as a guardian. He looks around 17-18 years old.

Kirika Hushima, the Latias


Kirika is the guardian of the blue orb of Hoenn and the guardian partner of Ayame. She is rather shy and quiet at first, but occasionally she will be a mouthful. Despite she is a legendary pokemon, she is still quite helpless, being a newborn transpecies. She looks around 16-17 years old.
