Woozi is a South Korean singer, K-Pop star, and producer signed to Pledis Entertainment in Gwangan-dong, Busan. Woozi is also recognized as the leader of the Seventeen Vocal Team of the Boys Music Group.

Early Life and Childhood

Woozi’s full name is Lee Ji Hoon. Lee was born on November 22, 1996, in Busan, South Korea. Lee has an older brother, but he has not shared the identity of his family member.

Likewise, he is 27 years old. He celebrates his birthday on November 22nd, which is why he has Scorpio as his Zodiac Sign. Aside from that, there is no further detail available about His parents, siblings, or early childhood life.

The singer attended Dongsuyeong Middle School in Busan, South Korea, and later graduated from Hanlim Multi Arts High School in 2015.

He grew up composing in his childhood, playing classical music regularly, and learning to play the clarinet and a few other musical instruments. His parents were supportive of their love, which is what made Him so successful in the end.

Professional life

He had always been interested in music and singing since he was a child, but his self-confidence in his vocal abilities was so low that he didn’t believe people when they told him he was a good singer.

He began his official career at a young age after passing his audition at Pledis Entertainment. The young boy began feeling a lot of gratitude for having such a chance.

He practiced hard every single day before his first debut in this field, just as he did when he was a child, and played classical music.

SEVENTEEN

He relocated to Seoul for training, and he also enrolled in the Hanlim Multi Arts High School. Lee is a member of SEVENTEEN and serves as a lead vocalist. However, the path that led him to that position was not easy.

Lee is a dynamic individual who writes all of the lyrics for the band’s songs. In addition to singing, dancing, and writing lyrics, Lee has collaborated on films such as Return of the Mafia, A Hard Day, and Proof of Innocence, to name a few.

Lee has starred in the TV shows School 2013, Blood, and Band of Sisters. Lee has also starred in television shows such as Big Debut Plan, One Fine Day, and others.

He was a member of the ‘Tempest’ group before the formation of the Seventeen. He is the album’s creator, he decides on the theme of the music, produces the songs, writes the lyrics, and considers the order in which they should be performed.

The musical arrangement of the group Seventeen is worked on by all of the members, but He is responsible for nearly 80 percent of it. When fans say their songs are nice, it makes him happier than anything else.

In one of the interviews, he said that he feels weighed down as the composer for the group Seventeen because he is concerned that if they fail, it will be his fault. According to the other seventeen group members, He, along with fellow Soonyoung, is the most dedicated member.
