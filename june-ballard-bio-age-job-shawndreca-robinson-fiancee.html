Meet June Ballard, “Kandi & The Gang” Star Shawndreca Robinson Fiancee

Shawndreca Robinson from “Kandi & The Gang” has a history with fellow star and Old Lady Gang manager Patrick Dallas. But, both of them have a fiance now. Patrick is engaged to Safari Foxe. Meanwhile, Shawndreca is with her fiancee June Ballard. Shawndreca and June got engaged in October 2019.

But, Patrick’s fiance Safari is not comfortable with him working with someone he had sex with, and the goes for June who isn’t thrilled about Shawndreca working close and getting platonic with Patrick.

Dating app Tinder is what brought Patrick and Shawndreca together. They celebrated their 2 years dating anniversary in October 2019. In February 2021, Shawndreca announced her wedding with June which is happening on 11 November 2022. Whether she had expected it or not but the reality star ended up finding someone she could spend her life with when she met June.

In February 2022, Shawndreca boasted that he fiance was her biggest supporter.

Besides the reality show, Shawn is also the owner of Shades by Dreca. She is also the operator of AirB&B rental houses and teaches swimming lessons. She is without a doubt a hustler who at one point worked 20 jobs until the COVID pandemic hit.

After the premiere of the show, June posted on his Instagram that nobody gave him the opportunity that she did and when the time comes that he shines, he knows that Shawn is the first person to thank first. He added, “Living in a selfish world, I conditioned myself to aim high and turn all of my dreams into reality… Out of my 1000 NOs, you were my 1 yes. February was a rough month, but we lived to see the day.”

June is a father of one son who turned 14 in September 2021. In the You42.com profile, it was mentioned that he has a wife so, he was could’ve been married to the mother of his son and got divorced.

June Ballard Age

In June Ballard’s Facebook, he shared that he was born in 1987. This makes him 34 years old.

June Ballard Job

June Ballard is working to pursue a career in music going by the rap name King June while working as an aspiring entrepreneur. He discovered hip hop at a young age and fell in love with it. Then he started working towards pursuing a career in hip-hoo. By the age of 11, he submitted his first demo to Slip N Slide Records.

At the age of 15, June and his friend started a rap group but tragedy struck when his friend got killed. He got discouraged but kept on working towards building a career in music.

He stated on a Facebook bio that he was “gearing to launch numerous of businesses in the near future”. Whereas his Instagram bio revealed that he is a producer also working as a director/writer and an actor. He also got into legal troubles which separated him from his beloved son.

June draws his inspiration from the rappers such as Master P, Jay-Z, 50Cent, and Jermaine Dupri.

In 2012, June launched his own “iDYMES Magazine” which featured artists such as 6lack, Future, The Game, Waka Flocka, Gucci Mane, K. Camp, and many others.

On top of that, June also happens to be an author who has published five books. He is also represented by Johnnie Cabbell who is a founder of Global Vision Talent Agency. He wrote, directed, and performed in the movie “Quad-City” in 2021. “King June” is currently pursuing his acting career and working on new music.
