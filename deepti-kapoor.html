Deepti Kapoor was born in the Uttar Pradesh town of Moradabad. Her family afterward moved to other locations. Her childhood was spent in Bombay, Dehradun, and Bahrain. At her school, she gained many wealthy friends. She started working as a journalist in Delhi while she was in her twenties. In addition to working as a journalist, she graduated from the University of Delhi in Journalism in 1997.
