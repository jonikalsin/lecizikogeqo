Overall Meaning

Duwap Kaine's song "Santa" starts with him boasting about his wealth and popularity. He proclaims that he has a "k" (likely referring to a gun) beside him that others are unaware of, and that he has a lot of girls interested in him, like Santa Claus has presents. He mocks other guys in the scene whom he calls "boozo" (a slang term for a foolish person) and "snake niggas." Duwap Kaine doesn't hesitate to assert his dominance, and he believes he is the original when it comes to style, rather than those who copy. He emphasizes that his success hasn't come easy; he has ridden around with criminals like Cito, and he always stays cautious.


Duwap Kaine repeats the chorus, in which he mocks the boyfriend of someone, as he is a "fucking lame" while signalling his own popularity and success. In the second verse, he talks about his street credibility and how he is not afraid to be aggressive. He likens himself to the fictional character "Goku" from the popular anime/manga "Dragon Ball Z," who can transform into a superhuman being at will. Duwap Kaine talks about wanting to stay true to himself and his roots, referencing the "trio" design on his pants that is likely linked to his upbringing or lifestyle. He talks about living a wild and erratic lifestyle, as he sips on lean and hangs out with women who are always watching him.


Overall, "Santa" is typical of Duwap Kaine's music in its themes of wealth, women, and street credibility, with thumping base and a punchy delivery style.
