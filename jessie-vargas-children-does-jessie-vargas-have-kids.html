Jessie Vargas is an American professional boxer who happens to be a former world champion in two weight classes, having held the WBA and IBO super lightweight titles in 2014, and the WBO welterweight title in 2016.

He was born in Los Angeles, California, the United States on May 10, 1989.

Jessie is 32 years old and stands at a height of 1.8 m.

He is a member of the Republican Party and has been nicknamed The New Generation. 

Jessie grew up in Los Angeles, Nevada, where he attended Palo Verde High School.

He is from a family of three siblings – two girls and a boy.

As of August 2021, he is ranked as the world’s eleventh best active welterweight by BoxRec.
