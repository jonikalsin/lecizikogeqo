Danielle Herrington Parents:- In this article, we are going to mention Danielle Herrington Parents. What are the names of Danielle Herrington’s parents? Who are Darryl and Theresa?

Danielle Herrington is an American model. She is known for her appearances in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue. She has been featured on the cover of the 2018 edition and became the 3rd African-American woman to appear on the cover.

She is associated with Elite Model Management Agency, Women Management Agency, Milk Management Agency, and Modelwerk Agency. 

Danielle Herrington Parents

Danielle Herrington was born to Darryl Herrington (Father) and Theresa Herrington (Mother) on 26 May 1993 in Compton, California, United States. She doesn’t have any siblings it means she is the only child of her parents.

Danielle Herrington Ethnicity, Age

Danielle Herrington is of African-American ethnicity. She is 29 years old as of 2023. She is 5 feet 10 inches tall. She has dark brown hair and Hazel eye.

Danielle Herrington Education, Early Life

She gained fame after becoming a resident of Brooklyn’s Bushwick neighborhood. She studied at Gahr High School in Cerritos, California and later she was homeschooled to focus on her modeling carer. She joined John Casablancas’ modeling school at the age of 10 or 11 and started modeling at the age of 13. She was featured in GQ in November 2016.
